Projects & Initiatives

💬 Magic Box

Anonymous Voice Platform for Youth

Vision: A safe, anonymous space where every voice is heard and valued.

🎯 Purpose

  • Allow students to express thoughts they can't share with friends or family
  • Create a supportive community where everyone feels seen and understood
  • Provide a judgment-free platform for authentic self-expression
  • Build empathy and connection through shared experiences

📍 Current Status

Pilot program running in high school (all 3 grades)

🚀 Expanding to Root & Shoot Global Network

💡 Why It Matters

  • Mental health & emotional well-being
  • Youth voice & agency
  • Community building through vulnerability
  • Creating safe spaces for authentic expression

🌱 Partnership

Collaborating with Root & Shoot - a global youth-led organization founded by Dr. Jane Goodall, dedicated to creating positive environmental and social change.
